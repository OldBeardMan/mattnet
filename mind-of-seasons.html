The Story

"Every album is a step. Every step tells a piece of the story."

Mind of Seasons is an exploration game that brings Matt Krupa's musical journey to life. Each level represents a different album - a unique world with its own atmosphere, mysteries, and secrets to uncover.

The Lost Kittens are scattered across all the worlds - lost pieces of emotions, memories, and moments from everyday life. Each kitten holds a part of the story. Your mission is to find them all.

Your guide through the worlds is Sprytek - a helper cat who knows all the secrets. With his help, you'll search through autumn forests, winter, cold lands of Norway, wild Iceland, and beyond - rescuing the lost kittens and piecing together the journey that connects all the albums into one story. When will the spring come?

Music & Game Connected

Mind of Seasons is more than a game - it's an interactive journey through Matt Krupa's entire musical catalog. Each level transforms an album into a playable world, with collectibles, secrets, and story elements drawn directly from the music.

Play each level while listening to its corresponding album for the full experience. As new albums are released, the game expands with new worlds to explore.
