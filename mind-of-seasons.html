The Story

"How can we find the album which we are inside?"

Welcome to the mysterious forest created by a lost musician. One day, he composed "Do Not Listen" and things got messy. Strange creatures started to spawn, and he disappeared without a trace.

Now, Sprytek the helper cat needs your assistance. Five kittens have scattered across the forest, and hidden collectibles may hold clues about the missing master. Can you find them all and uncover the truth?

But remember - don't listen to the creatures. They whisper things you don't want to hear...

Connected to the Album

Mind of Seasons takes place inside the world of In the Autumn Forest - Matt Krupa's first album released to the public. The collectibles you find are items from the album: the old printer that started it all, the mysterious Do Not Listen composition, the Return sign, and more...

Play the game while listening to the album for the full experience.
