Who is Matt?

The story behind the music

Hi, I'm Matt Krupa - a musician and composer from Poland. I create music that tells stories - from cozy forests through frozen Nordic landscapes to a spring at last. My albums are chapters of one bigger journey, and my game Mind of Seasons brings these worlds to life.

Genres: Ambient | Electronic | Acoustic | Instrumental

The Journey

  • The Beginning: From Guitar Hero to Real Guitar - It all started when I was 14 and loved playing Guitar Hero. I asked my brother to teach me guitar, but he convinced me that bass would be easier to learn. So I picked up the bass and soon joined a local band where I learned to play by ear.
  • 2020: First Covers - I started recording covers at home, experimenting with whatever equipment I had. My first releases were acoustic covers - simple recordings that taught me the basics of home production. I was figuring things out one step at a time.
  • 2020: In The Autumn Forest - After getting a MIDI controller and Ableton, I decided to compose my first real album. Inspired by C418's atmospheric music, I created 7 tracks that became In The Autumn Forest - the beginning of a bigger story.
  • 2021-2022: Nordic Countries - I wanted to push further - more melodies, better production, bigger soundscapes. The Nordic Countries albums (Norway, Iceland, Winter) expanded the musical universe with cold, atmospheric journeys through imaginary northern lands.
  • 2023-2024: Cats & Lost Kittens - I started exploring electronic music more deeply - remixing old tracks, creating new beats, and releasing the "Cats" series. This led to Lost Kittens - an album of tracks scattered across all my previous albums, finally brought together.
  • Now: Mind of Seasons - All the albums are connected by one story - and now that story is becoming a game. Mind of Seasons transforms each album into a playable world where you can explore, find lost kittens, and discover the secrets hidden in the music.
  • 2025-2026: Spring at Last - After years of autumn, winter, and cold Nordic lands... spring is finally coming.
