Light of Spring

Journey coming to an end

Epilogue for a 6-year journey.

Light of Spring is an album that concludes the story told through my previous albums, starting from In The Autumn Forest! It's a transition from this story, which had been going on in my mind, into reality! It's about everyday life, nature, work and the miracle of life.

The album starts with the first song ever created for it. ,,Spring at Last" was my first choice for the album name, but after 3 years in the making I decided it should reflect the light more. By the way, the song is one of 3 songs with lyrics on the album and is a continuation of the song ,,Cat 10 - Journey", where I sing that ,,The Journey is ending", and here in ,,Spring at Last" it finally ends: ,,My purposes are being fulfilled".

The second song, ,,Back from the Coldest Dream", is a beautiful continuation of the previous one and an epilogue for the series. I sing there about coming back to the forest (from the In The Autumn Forest album), but now it's all colorful, as the spring has come. It references the Nordic Countries project, the first album ,,In The Autumn Forest", the Do Not Listen II single, the song Ten (from the Blooming Cactus band), Pls Come from the Winter album, Lost Kittens of course, and my beloved cat Sprytek. So yeah, I can say this is the ending song...

The next song, ,,Morning Train", starts a series of songs on the album about work. Because in the previous song I sing ,,To reality return", so here is the reality, lmao. Work. This song was composed very early in the morning while I was on a train to work. I didn't enjoy those trips, but I tried to be optimistic, and I hope this song reflects that.

The fourth song, Little Steps, starts another series of songs, all being remixes of the longest song on the album, Little Light, which is the biggest, most demanding and most beloved song I have ever created. It is about a very tiny human being and its very tiny little steps, which take it slowly further and further, exploring more and more. You can hear a recording of birds singing in my garden.

The fifth song, ,,Mist", is one of the first songs created for the album. It is about trust in general. I sing that I don't really know where I'm heading, but ,,I'm guided only by trust".

The next song, ,,I wanna go home", references many jobs I had in previous years - exhausting, boring jobs... And all I could think about was just going home.

,,Little Light" is the longest song on the album; every minute of it represents a different time period. This was the most demanding song I have ever created, and I love it. It was originally created for a movie (which I will keep private) and is, in itself, a present for one person.

The eighth song on the album, ,,Afternoon Train", was my way to express the emotions after a long and demanding day at work. It was created on a train as well. As the song progresses, it gets a bit happier. I wanted to express tiredness, but also the relief that the day is coming to an end.

,,Little Bed" is another remix of ,,Little Light". I really wanted to expand ,,Little Light" into other ways of making it, and I wanted to make it the main theme of the album. It is about peaceful dreams.

,,1220 Train" references a specific train that always gave me hope after a long day at work. It was heading to the city I lived in, and reading the city's name I felt very relieved that I was coming back.

,,Light of Spring" was created when I was just chilling on the sofa in my apartment, playing a few notes for a person I love. It simply felt right to make it the album's title song!

The next song, ,,Let me go home", is about stress and fear. When I'm in a place where I don't feel safe and don't have anyone I can hold onto, I just wanna go home, to a safe place, to rest and quit the noise.

The last song (at least on the digital release) is ,,The Storm", created in 2023 and reworked in 2025. It has a recording of a storm in the background. I don't have a meaning for this song, as - at the time of writing this - I don't have a plan for what to do next with my music...

There are two more songs available only on the CD and Vinyl releases of the record, but they are TOO PERSONAL to be thrown onto the internet. I think they are the most beautiful ones, as someone else is featured on them.
